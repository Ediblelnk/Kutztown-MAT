\subsection*{Problem 1.}
Let $V = \bb{R}^+ \times \bb{R}$ be a set. In other words, every element of $V$ is in the form $(u_1, u_2), \twhere u_1~\in \bb{R}^+ \tand u_2 \in \bb{R}$. For all $(u_1,u_2) \tand (v_1,v_2) \in V$, and for all $k \in \bb{R}$,
\[
  (u_1,u_2) \oplus (v_1,v_2) = (2u_1v_1, u_2 + v_2 - 3) \tand k \odot (u_1,v_1) = (u_1^k, ku_2).
\]
Verify the axioms 4, 5, and 7.
